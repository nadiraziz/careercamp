CAREER CAMPUS ACADEMY

CAREER CAMPUS was established with an aim to create skilled professionals as per the current industrial requirements and guide them to achieve their career success through our unique integrated programs. Career Campus is the only campus college in Kerala where studies of Airlines, Language and Management Studies and related courses are imparted. The campus is established since 2000. We conduct in our institute various job oriented and attractive courses suited for IT Courses, Management Degree/Diploma Courses, Different Types of Language Courses, Aviation, Tourism and Hospitality sectors.

Classes are handled by experts in relevant field. The Management encourages all attempts and innovations to mold out the students self motivated and to create self confidence. They get opportunities for their exposure in their academic performance as well as extra curricular activities. We run a dynamic recruitment cell to enable leading industries to pick the most suitable candidates, and fit to their needs.

We set and maintain new benchmark in management studies delivering a portfolio of various certification in Diploma, graduation and post graduation levels and various State Government university approved by UGC. We have a strong pedigree in development of quality education and we always set international standard for management methodologies.
